What We Offer

At Stitch & Style, we pride ourselves on delivering high-quality embroidery services with meticulous attention to detail and exceptional craftsmanship.

Custom Designs

Transform your ideas into beautiful embroidered creations with our custom design service. Our team of skilled designers works closely with you to create unique patterns that perfectly capture your vision.

Whether you have a sketch, logo, or just a concept in mind, we can help bring it to life with precision and creativity.

Professional Design Consultation - Our experts will guide you through the design process, offering suggestions and advice to optimize your embroidery.
Digital Proof Approval - Review and approve your design before production begins to ensure it meets your expectations.
Unlimited Revisions - We'll work with you until your design is perfect, with no limit on revision rounds.

Logo Embroidery

Showcase your brand identity with professionally embroidered logos on various materials. Our logo embroidery service is perfect for corporate uniforms, promotional items, and merchandise.

We ensure consistent quality and accurate color matching to maintain your brand standards across all embroidered items.

Logo Digitization - We convert your logo into an embroidery-ready digital format optimized for stitching.
Color Matching - Precise thread color selection to match your brand colors accurately.
Multiple Placement Options - Apply your logo on various positions including chest, sleeve, back, and more.
